27 - 29 Jun – New York

NYC Gay Pride

The New York City Gay Pride is the originall Christopher Street Day, home of the Stonewall gay bar.
3 - 6 Jul – Madrid

Gay Pride / Orgullo Madrid

The gay pide - or Orgullo in Spanish - in Madrid is one of the biggest and most popular prides in Europe. The parade is attended by 1.500.000 people every year. The street parties at Plaza Chueca attracted 300.000 people.
4 - 6 Jul – Cologne

Cologne Gay Pride - CSD

The very popular gay pride / CSD Christopher Street Day in Cologne (KÃ¶ln / Koeln) which attracts hundred of thousands of gay men and women from all over Europe.
21 Jul – Tilburg

Roze Maandag

One of the biggest gay events in Holland, the Roze Maandag is a special gay Monday on the biggest fairground of Europe. Many special parties all over town, and many, many gay guys on the streets. Roze Maandag is not to be missed!
22 - 25 Aug – Manchester

Manchester Gay Pride

Manchester Gay Pride is maybe not that well known, but according to Nighttours it is one of the best in Europe ! All bars and clubs are located in the Gay Village around Canalstreet. During pride the whole block is sealed off.
