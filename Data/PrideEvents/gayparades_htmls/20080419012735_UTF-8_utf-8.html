After Montreal in 2006, Copenhagen will be the next city to host the World OutGames. From 25 July till 2 August 2009, thousands of gay and lesbian athletes will compete in the Danish capital.

Gay Games VIII Cologne 2010
Between July 31st and August 7th in 2010 the Gay Games VIII will take place in Cologne, Germany. Around 12.000 participants from all over the world are expected to take part in this sport and cultural event.
