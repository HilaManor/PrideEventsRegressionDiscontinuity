Gay Pride
Saturday 24 June will be the day of the Paris Pride 2006. More than 700.000 people will be in the streets of Paris to make this the biggest French Pride Parade. The parade starts at Montparnasse in the direction of Bastille.

Antwerp will host the 11th European Gay and Lesbian Sports Federation Eurogames. The biggest athletic event for lesbians, gays, bisexuals and transgender people in Europe. From 12 to 15 July, Antwerp will welcome about 3.000 participants and 10.000 visitors.
Berlin
16 - 24 Jul 2007
Pride Week / CSD
Christopher Street Day is Berlin's gay pride. It is the end of Pride week. The big parade on the last Saturday of Pride week will follow a route from the Kurfurstendamm to the Siegesäule. At night a big Pride party will close CSD.
Stockholm
30 Jul - 5 Aug 2007
Gaypride
Since the start in 1998 Stockholm Pride has grown to become one of Stockholm's largest annual festivals and also the largest Pride celebration in Scandinavia. Every year Stockholm Pride attracts visitors from Sweden and the rest of the world.

Gay Pride
Amsterdam Gay Pride is one of the most special and the biggest pride events around the world. During the first weekend of August, you can always enjoy a series of circuit parties, street parties and (of course) the world's only gay Canal Parade.
Manchester
24 - 27 Aug 2007
Gay Pride
Manchester Pride is maybe not that well known, but according to Nighttours it is one of the best in Europe ! All bars and clubs are located in the Gay Village around Canalstreet. During pride the whole block is sealed off.
