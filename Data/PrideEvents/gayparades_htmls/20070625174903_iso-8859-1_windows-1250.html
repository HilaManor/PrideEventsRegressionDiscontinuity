Highlights

EuroGames Antwerp
3 days of sports and fun in Antwerp. EuroGames will attract gays and lesbians from all over Europe.
  • More info
One Mighty Party Paris
One Mighty Weekend at The Walt Disney World® Resort Orlando, will come to Europe. On June 29 and 30 2007, The Disneyland Resort Paris will be the site for One Mighty Party Paris.
  • More info
Orgullo Gay Euro Pride
Massive gay pride celebration in the (small) streets of gay area Chueca, attracting millions of visitors. 2007 is also EuroPride.
  • More info
Amsterdam Gay Pride
This very popular pride takes place the first weekend of August. Highlights are the floating canal parade, outdoor street parties and big parties like Whiteparty, Rapido and many more.
  • More info
Manchester Pride
This pride always takes place the last weekend of August and is the most popular gay pride in the UK and one of our favorites!
  • More info
World OutGames Copenhagen 2009
After Montreal in 2006, Copenhagen will be the next city to host the World OutGames. Exact dates are to be announced yet, but end of July, beginning of August (during Pride) will be the most obvious time.
  • More info
Gay Games VIII Cologne 2010
Between July 31st and August 7th in 2010 the Gay Games VIII will take place in Cologne, Germany. Around 12.000 participants from all over the world are expected to take part in this sport and cultural event.
  • More info
