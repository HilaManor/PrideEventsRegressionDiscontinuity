Highlights

12 - 19 Feb – Fort Lauderdale – RSVP Caribbean Cruise

All gay cruise by RSVP Vacations. RSVP is similar to Atlantis, but a bit more laid back. You can party all night long if you want, but the focus is more on a well rounded vacation experience. RSVP has a fun and friendly crowd where all body types, personalities and ages are comfortable and welcomed.

17 - 21 Feb – Rio de Janeiro – Carnival Rio

The world most famous Carnival is creating an exploding vibe along Rio. With different gay club and street parties (Ipanema) and of course the world famous samba parades at the Sambadrome.

3 Mar – Sydney – Mardi Gras Parade

Mardi Gras Parade has grown to become a major cultural event and its appeal stretches well beyond Sydney's gay community and its close supporters.

5 - 10 Apr – Berlin – BLF | ECMC Leather & Fetish Week

Berlin turns fetish for the long Easter Weekend. Gays from all over Europe pack there leather , rubber, uniform or other fetish clothing and fly to Berlin for the BLF | ECMC Leather & Fetish WEEK.

30 Apr – Amsterdam – Queensday

The Dutch national holiday where we celebrate the Queens birthday. See our Queensday (Koninginnedag) special.
