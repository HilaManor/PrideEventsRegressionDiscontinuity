Highlights

5 Mar 2011 – Sydney – Mardi Gras Parade

Mardi Gras Parade has grown to become a major cultural event and its appeal stretches well beyond Sydney's gay community and its close supporters.

5 - 8 Mar 2011 – Rio de Janeiro – Carnival Rio

The world most famous Carnival is creating a exploding vibe along Rio. With different gay club and street parties (Ipanema) and of course the world famous samba parades at the Sambadrome.

21 - 26 Avr 2011 – Berlin – BLF | ECMC Leather & Fetish Week

Berlin turns fetish for the long Easter Weekend. Gays from all over Europe pack there leather , rubber, uniform or other fetish clothing and fly to Berlin for the BLF | ECMC Leather & Fetish WEEK. Lots of parties and events are on the agenda this weekend including Fetish- and Sexpartys, the traditional Leatherdinner, the Townrally, the Leatherboat, the BLF Gay Bowling Cup and of course the HIGHLIGHT, the Mega Leather- and Fetishparty with the official contest of the "German Mr. Leather" 2010.

30 Avr 2011 – Amsterdam – Queensday

The Dutch national holiday where we celebrate the Queens birthday. See our Queensday (Koninginnedag) special.

6 - 15 Mai 2011 – Gran Canaria – Gay Pride Maspalomas

The Maspalomas Gay Pride has become the second largest of Spain (after the one in Madrid). For a week, different cultural and political activities and drag shows are held on the island.
