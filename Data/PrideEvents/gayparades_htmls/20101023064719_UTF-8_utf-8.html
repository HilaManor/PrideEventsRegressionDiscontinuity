Highlights

28 - 31 Oct – Amsterdam – Get Ruff! Leather Pride Weekend

Amsterdam Leather Pride got a make over and Gets Ruff! Leather and fetish men from all over Europe will take the city for this annual fetish event. With special fetish parties at the leather bars, in Club Church and the main party Ruff Mega Party.

29 - 31 Oct – Brussels – La Demence 21th Anniversary Weekend

European Circuit Party celebrates its 21th Anniversary Weekend with a Classic Demence in Club Fuse on Friday, Water Park Demence on Saturday and a Main Anniversary Party on Sunday. With After on Monday morning. Check out these pages for more info.

5 Mar 2011 – Sydney – Mardi Gras Parade

Mardi Gras Parade has grown to become a major cultural event and its appeal stretches well beyond Sydney's gay community and its close supporters.

5 - 8 Mar 2011 – Rio de Janeiro – Carnival Rio

The world most famous Carnival is creating a exploding vibe along Rio. With different gay club and street parties (Ipanema) and of course the world famous samba parades at the Sambadrome.

30 Apr 2011 – Amsterdam – Queensday

The Dutch national holiday where we celebrate the Queens birthday. See our Queensday (Koninginnedag) special.
