Highlights

23 - 26 Aug – Manchester

Manchester Gay Pride

Manchester Gay Pride is maybe not that well known, but according to Nighttours it is one of the best in Europe ! All bars and clubs are located in the Gay Village around Canalstreet. During pride the whole block is sealed off.
14 - 15 Sep – Berlin

Folsom Europe

European edition of infamous Falsom street party. Folsom Europe brings many fetish lovers from all over Europe, and others, to Berlin to celebrate a unique weekend of fun, fetish and .....
29 Sep – San Francisco

Folsom Street Fair

The Folsom Street Fair takes place during San Francisco's Leather Pride Week, eight days of leather at the end of September. This is a week's worth of intense activity, with the Folsom Street Fair being the crowning finale to a festive time!
25 - 27 Oct – Amsterdam

Get Ruff! Leather Pride Weekend

Amsterdam Leather Pride got a make over and Gets Ruff! Leather and fetish men from all over Europe will take the city for this annual fetish event. With special fetish parties at the leather bars, in Club Church and the main party Ruff Mega Party.
2 - 9 Feb 2014 – Fort Lauderdale

Atlantis Caribbean all gay cruise

All gay cruise through the Caribbean Sea by Atlantis Events on the beautiful Celebrity Silhouette, with a capacity of 2800 guys!
