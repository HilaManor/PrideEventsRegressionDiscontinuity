Highlights

3 - 13 May – Gran Canaria

Gay Pride Maspalomas

Maspalomas Gay Pride has become the second largest gay event of Spain (after the one in Madrid). For a week, different cultural and political activities and drag shows are held on the island.
20 May – New York

NY AIDS Walk

Since 1986, AIDS Walk New York has raised more than $105 million for HIV programs and services in the tri-state area, and has grown into the largest AIDS fundraising event in the world.
29 May - 3 Jun – Orlando

Disney Gay Days

Since 1991, gay men and lesbians from around the world have converged in and around Walt Disney World in Orlando, Florida for the Disney Gay Days, an entire weekend of exciting events centered around area theme parks and attractions.
3 Jun – Sao Paulo

Gay Pride Sao Paulo

The Parada do Orgulho GLBT de Sao Paulo, in Portuguese, has grown from 2,000 participants in 1997 to 2,5 million in 2006, when they made to the Guinness Book, for the world’s biggest Gay Pride celebration. 4 million people are expected for coming year.
14 - 18 Jun – Sitges

Gay Pride

Sitges, the popular gay destination south of Barcelona, has now its own gay pride. With a big street parade on Saturday.
